What is Hampshire?

Hampshire is a neighborhood of more than 250 houses in a park-like, rural setting. With more than 60 acres of green space, 3+ miles of trails, numerous ponds, and a 15+ acre forest, Hampshire's spacious, rural setting is a refreshing change of pace.

Amenities Galore

A community pool, a kiddie pool, a playground, and pickleball and basketball courts provide endless possibilities for year-round outdoor activities.

Trail System

Hampshire's extensive trail system connects directly to the Zionsville Rail Trail, providing bicycle-friendly access throughout the area.

Family Friendly

With top-rated Zionsville schools within walking distance and numerous family amenities, Hampshire is perfect for families of all sizes.

ACR Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the Architectural Change Request process

What changes require an ACR?

+

Most exterior modifications including additions, decks, fences, landscaping changes, exterior paint colors, roofing materials, and driveway modifications require ACR approval.

How long does ACR approval take?

+

The board typically reviews ACR submissions within 30 days of receipt. Complex requests may require additional time for thorough evaluation.

What information should I include in my ACR?

+

Include detailed plans, material specifications, color samples, contractor information, and timeline. Photos of the current area are also helpful.

Is there a fee for ACR submissions?

+

Contact the board for current fee information. Some minor changes may not require a fee, while major modifications typically do.
