Enforcement and Compliance

Violation Process

When violations are identified, residents receive written notice with details about the issue and timeframe for correction. The board works with homeowners to resolve issues cooperatively.

Appeals Process

Residents have the right to appeal any violation notice or fine. Appeals are reviewed by the board during regular meetings, and residents may present their case in person.
